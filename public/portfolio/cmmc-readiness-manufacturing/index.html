CMMC Level 2 Readiness for a Defense Subcontractor

Manufacturing subcontractor, Colorado Springs

110
controls assessed
18
days to a working roadmap
9
critical gaps closed in phase 1

The problem

A 40-person manufacturing subcontractor needed to demonstrate CMMC Level 2 readiness to keep bidding on defense contracts, but had no documented System Security Plan and no idea how many of the 110 NIST 800-171 controls they actually met.

The solution

I ran a full gap assessment against all 110 controls, built out their SSP from scratch, and delivered a prioritized 90-day POA&M so their team could fix the highest-risk gaps first without freezing operations.

The engagement started with a two-day discovery sprint mapping their environment against every control family — access control, incident response, media protection, and so on. From there, the SSP and POA&M gave their leadership team something concrete to bring to their prime contractor: not just “we’re working on it,” but a dated plan with owners attached to each gap.
