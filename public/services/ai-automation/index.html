AI & Process Automation

Practical AI automation for small businesses in Colorado Springs, CO — automating reporting, intake, and approvals so your team spends time on judgment calls, not busywork.

Book a free discovery call → Get a quote

Most “AI automation” pitches are vague. Mine isn’t: I look at the specific spreadsheets, forms, and approval chains that eat your team’s week, and automate the parts that don’t need a human judgment call.

Because I come from a security background, every automation is also reviewed for the obvious failure modes — what happens if this breaks, what data it touches, and who can see it.

Where this tends to pay off fastest

  • Reporting that gets manually assembled from multiple sources every week
  • Data entry between systems that don’t talk to each other
  • Approval or intake workflows with a lot of email back-and-forth
  • Customer or client follow-ups that fall through the cracks

How it’s delivered

I start with a short workflow audit, build the automation, and hand it off with documentation your team can actually maintain — plus an option for an ongoing retainer if you’d rather I keep an eye on it.
