I design and build websites the way a security engineer builds a website: fast by default, hardened by default, and without the plugin bloat that turns a marketing site into an attack surface.

Whether it's a one-page portfolio, a full marketing site, or a product front-end, everything is hand-coded — no page builder overhead, no unnecessary dependencies, no six-second load times.

## What you get

A site that loads in under a second, reads clearly on a phone, and doesn't hand an attacker an easy way in through an outdated plugin. Built on Hugo for pure marketing sites, or React when the project needs more interactivity.

## Who this is for

Small businesses and professional service firms in Colorado Springs who are tired of their current site being slow, dated, or a liability — and who want the same person who builds the site to also understand how to keep it secure.
