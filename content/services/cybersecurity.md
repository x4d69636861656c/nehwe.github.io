Most small businesses don't need a full internal security team — they need someone senior to tell them, clearly, where the actual risk is and what to fix first.

I work with small and mid-sized businesses in Colorado Springs who need real security coverage without standing up an internal team, and with teams who already have one and want an outside set of eyes.

## Frameworks I work against

- **NIST 800-171 & CSF** — gap assessments and remediation roadmaps
- **CMMC** — readiness assessments for defense contractors and subcontractors
- **ISO 27001** — ISMS gap analysis and audit preparation
- **PCI DSS** — scoping, SAQ support, and remediation for payment environments
- **HIPAA** — Security Rule risk assessments for covered entities and associates

## How an engagement runs

Every engagement starts with scoping — mapping your assets and agreeing exactly what's in bounds before anything is touched. From there it's automated scanning, manually validated, so every finding that reaches you has actually been confirmed. You get a prioritized report and a walkthrough call, in plain language.
